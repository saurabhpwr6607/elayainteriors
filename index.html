About Us

Elaya Interiors brings timeless elegance to every space we design

Designing spaces that reflect who you are. At Elaya Interiors, we believe that great design is not just about aesthetics — it’s about creating environments that elevate the way you live and work. Based in India, we specialize in residential and commercial interiors, turnkey projects, and custom furniture, offering complete, end-to-end solutions with a focus on quality, creativity, and functionality.

Whether it’s a cozy home, a dynamic office, or a retail space, our team of designers and craftsmen collaborate closely with clients to bring their vision to life — with precision, innovation, and attention to every detail. With a passion for materials, a flair for modern yet timeless design, and a hands-on approach to execution, Elaya Interiors ensures each project is uniquely tailored to suit your lifestyle or brand identity. “We don’t just design spaces — we shape experiences.”

Process/how we work

Turning visions into reality

Consultation & Free Site Visit

We begin with an initial consultation to understand your needs, preferences, budget, and lifestyle or brand. A site visit allows us to take measurements, assess the space, and discuss possibilities.
Goal: Understand your vision + evaluate space potential.

Concept Design & Planning

Based on your inputs, we create mood boards, material palettes, layout options, and 3D design concepts to help you visualize the transformation.
Goal: Turn ideas into an actionable design direction

Detailed Estimation & Proposal

Once the concept is approved, we prepare a detailed BOQ (Bill of Quantities), material specification sheet, and a transparent project quote.
Goal: Clear scope, cost, and timeline.

Execution & Project Management

Our team manages every aspect of the build — from civil work and electrical to furniture, finishes, and final styling. With regular updates, site supervision, and quality checks, we ensure smooth progress.
Goal: Hassle-free, high-quality execution.

Custom Furniture Installation

If opted, we manufacture and install custom furniture pieces designed specifically for your space. All items are made in-house with premium materials and expert craftsmanship.
Goal: Perfect fit + functional elegance

Final Styling & Handover

We complete the finishing touches — cleaning, decor placement, final inspection — and walk you through your transformed space.
Goal: Ready-to-live or ready-to-use delivery.

After-Support & Warranty

We stay in touch even after the handover. Our team is available for touch-ups, warranty support, and future upgrades.
Goal: Long-term trust and client satisfaction.
